Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the application (“the App”) handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal identifiers (such as name, email, or phone number) are collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No analytics or tracking technologies are used

The App operates entirely on your device.

2. Local Data

Any data created while using the App (such as selected cities, time settings, or reminders) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. System-Level Data

The operating system may collect limited technical information (such as crash logs or device diagnostics). This data is handled by the platform provider and is not accessible to the App developer.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect user data.

5. Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
